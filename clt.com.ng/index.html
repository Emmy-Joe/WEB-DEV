03.

Who We Are

Crystallink Tutorials and Technologies, registered by the Nigerian Government, has been delivering top-notch educational services since January 2016. Our students have excelled, with many attending prestigious institutions in Nigeria and abroad. We follow the latest JAMB and WAEC syllabuses and the approved Senior secondary school curriculum, ensuring quality education with experienced Teachers.

Our comprehensive classroom tutorials which cover all JAMB and WAEC subjects, holds in a conducive learning environment at our center. Additionally, our Skills acquisition program equip young individuals to be self-reliant and financially independent as they transition to higher education.

We offer well-organized, intensive online tutorials and face-to-face sessions. We also provide practical hands-on digital skills training in computer programming, website development, data analysis, and more. Furthermore, we develop and host stunning, responsive, and SEO-optimized websites for clients, from simple sites to complex web apps.
